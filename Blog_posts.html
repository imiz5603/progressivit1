Websites that adapt to any device

Ever accessed a website while on the go, and found it completely jumbled? Picutres over text, links wrapping over to the next line, or off the page completely! These are examples of bad website responsiveness.

With a framework known as Bootstrap, websites are able to adapt and respond to any device, no matter the size of the screen. All websites designed by Progressiv incorporate the Bootstrap framework, to ensure a pleasurable viewing experience for your customers.

Examples of varying website responsiveness

Bad:

Involves pinching to zoom, swiping to see all text on the same line

Good:

All information available and on-screen immedietly, multiple pictures will resize procede one another accordingly.
